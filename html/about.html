All about me!

Hey y'all! I was born in Mesa, Arizona, however, I call my home San Antonio, Texas. I lived there for almost 14 years before moving to BYU for the summer semester this year (2021). I have a family of two wonderful parents and 4 younger siblings. I am grateful for all of their love and support. I enjoy spending time creating music on the cello and piano, hand-lettering, eating Chick-Fil-A, and sharing my testimony of Jesus Christ with friends and family. I would say my three greatest strengths are hard work, compassion, and optimism.
